Websites


Figma

Reason: Figma is one of the most popular collaborative design tools. It is quite handy, easy to use and friendly for teamwork. I would recommend it when you have a team work design.

Designercize

Designercize could generate different whiteboard challenges in seconds. It is a great tool for UX designers who hunt for jobs. They are able to practice their whiteboard skills.

Bestfolios

It includes all great portfolios, resumes and case studies in the world. It is a great place to get some inspiration on your portfolio.

Cofolios

Cofolios is a collection of UX design portfolios of designers who intern at top tech companies. If you are a UX designer looking for internship opportunities, Cofolios is a resource for you!

Openpeeps

It is an illustration library which can help a lot in making story boards. The library works like building blocks made of vector arms, legs, and emotions. You can mix these elements to create different Peeps.

Medium

Medium is an open platform where readers find dynamic thinking, and where expert and undiscovered voices can share their writing on any topic.Medium works well when you are preparing your interviews.
